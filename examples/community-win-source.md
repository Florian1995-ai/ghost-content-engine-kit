# Example Source: Redacted Community Win

## Category

Community win.

## Raw Context To Redact

Member Name posted a monetary win: they sold a simple website project for $3,000, with half upfront and half after delivery.

## Take

The public article should not expose the member. The useful lesson is the offer pattern: a simple first-client bridge can work when the buyer understands the immediate next step and the risk feels manageable.

## Angles

- Why half upfront increases commitment.
